Wedding Receptions

Here for your big day

Planning a wedding can be hectic. To put you at ease and help make this day truly unforgettable, we rent our beautiful common areas as reception venues. Each venue comes with a simple catering menu that presents several distinct and delicious options (Mediterranean, Texas barbecue, and Thai), as well as kitchen access if you'd prefer that to catering. Specific layout and decoration options vary by location, so be sure to contact us today to investigate how we can be a part of the beginning of your 'forever'.

Mediterranean

This menu includes salads, pastas, pizza, and plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables.

Texas barbecue

If you're seeking meat, beans, and a hearty meal, look no further.

Thai

This exotic option showcases bright (and occasionally burning) Oriental flavors.
