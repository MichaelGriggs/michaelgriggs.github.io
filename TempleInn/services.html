Services we offer

Each room we rent offers:

  • 1 or more beds
  • Half or full bathroom
  • Complimentary breakfast
  • Access to exercise/workout room and pool
  • High-speed wifi and smart TV in room
  • Parking space and temple shuttle
  • Family History Consultation service in lobby

In addition, the following are offered in the common areas of our locations:

  • Access to bathrooms and changing rooms for travelers
  • Playroom and affordable short-term childcare for temple patrons
  • Inviting reception area with temple-themed decor
  • Wedding Reception space easily configurable for catering and serving
  • Integrated family search center to support temple work
