Full-Time Missionaries

We respect your full-time service in the temple.

As full-time temple missionaries, you make sacrifices of time and resources to serve in the way you do. We believe your service shouldn't be hampered by difficulty finding a place to stay, or by finding lodging that doesn't reflect the nature of your service. As a result, we offer special discounts on kitchenette suites to full-time temple missionaries to do our part to support you as you serve in your unique capacities.


Our suites provide the ideal atmosphere for rest and reflection. Features include:

  • Prints of classic LDS oil paintings
  • LED lights and lamps with warm tones and no buzz
  • Excellent sound insulation from exterior road noise, etc.
  • Smart TV preset to listen to sacred music
  • Iron and ironing board to press linen fabrics

Kitchenettes also include:

  • 4-burner stove
  • Basic utensils
  • Table settings for 4
  • Miniature refrigerator (3.3 Cu Ft.)
  • Microwave oven
  • Table and chairs
  • Sink and dish drying rack
